Auto-Update Delivery — Elevated Error Rate

A subset of CrestDesk clients experienced failed or timed-out update checks between 13:08 and 14:23 UTC. The root cause has been identified and a fix deployed. Error rates have returned to baseline. We are monitoring to confirm full resolution before closing this incident.

MonitoringFix deployed

IdentifiedRoot cause confirmed
Root cause identified as connection pool exhaustion on the update-delivery-eu-west-2 node group, triggered by a background metadata index rebuild that was consuming an unexpectedly large number of database connections. A configuration change is being prepared and will be deployed within the next 30–40 minutes.
